# Workflow & Prompts — UGC-v1

## Creative strategy

The core strategy was to make the ad feel like a normal bedtime mirror-talk video, not a polished supplement ad.

The structure:

1. **Identity reframe** — “I thought I was low energy.”
2. **Sleep insight** — “8 hours does not always mean good recovery.”
3. **Routine bridge** — “I started paying attention to how I wind down.”
4. **Product moment** — Gummy Dummy enters as part of the routine.
5. **Personality close** — “Not a sleep hack… Toodles.”

## Scene 1 — Hook

**Voiceover:**  
“Okay story time. I always thought I was just a low energy person — turns out I was just a bad sleeper.”

**Image prompt:**  
Vertical 9:16 realistic UGC-style phone shot of a put-together woman in her late 20s at a bedroom vanity mirror at night, softly removing makeup with cleansing balm, warm bedside lamp lighting, casual bedtime GRWM atmosphere, skincare products scattered naturally on the vanity, slightly imperfect phone framing, mirror reflection visible, cozy realistic TikTok aesthetic, no polished commercial look, natural skin texture, calm tired expression.

**Motion prompt:**  
Static phone camera. Woman gently removes makeup while talking to camera through the mirror. Natural hand movement, soft blinking, subtle facial expressions, relaxed bedtime energy. No dramatic motion, no commercial camera movement.

## Scene 2 — Sleep quality insight

**Voiceover:**  
“Apparently you can sleep 8 hours and still wake up exhausted. It’s about quality, not just duration.”

**Image prompt:**  
Realistic vertical phone-shot scene of the same woman at a vanity mirror, halfway through removing makeup, warm dim lamp light, cozy bedroom bathroom setting, natural skincare routine, candid TikTok GRWM style, woman looking at her mirror reflection while speaking, understated expression of realization, intimate nighttime mood, no studio lighting.

**Motion prompt:**  
Continue the same shot. Woman wipes her cheek with a cotton pad, glances at herself in the mirror, then back toward the phone reflection. Keep movement small and authentic.

## Scene 3 — Personal bridge

**Voiceover:**  
“Didn’t figure that out until I started paying attention to how I was winding down.”

**Image prompt:**  
Candid vertical UGC bedroom vanity shot, same late-20s woman in a bedtime routine, warm lamp lighting, bare face partially visible after makeup removal, still in casual TikTok mirror-talk mode, glancing down thoughtfully at skincare and supplements on the vanity while continuing to speak, relaxed natural posture, cozy imperfect real-life UGC aesthetic, soft shadows, intimate nighttime atmosphere.

**Motion prompt:**  
Woman continues speaking naturally in a self-shot UGC style, glances down at the vanity mid-thought, lightly moves or reaches toward a skincare item, then continues her sentence without breaking the conversational flow. Keep the pause subtle, slightly awkward, and real, like an unscripted thought during storytime.

## Scene 4 — Product moment

**Voiceover:**  
“That’s when I found Gummy Dummy — a chewable supplement that helps you wind down and sleep better.”

**Image prompt:**  
Realistic vertical UGC phone shot of the same woman at her vanity during a nighttime GRWM routine, warm lamp lighting, natural skincare bottles and supplements on the vanity, she casually holds a small sleep gummy product labeled Gummy Dummy while continuing to talk, product visible but not overly staged, candid self-shot TikTok feel, soft shadows, cozy bedroom vanity, authentic non-commercial composition.

**Motion prompt:**  
Woman reaches toward the vanity, picks up the Gummy Dummy sleep gummy bottle naturally, and holds it in frame while continuing her sentence in a self-shot UGC style. The bottle should feel like part of her normal routine, not a commercial reveal. No zoom, no exaggerated smile, no product hero hold.

## Scene 5 — Tagline

**Voiceover:**  
“Not a sleep hack. It’s just a gummy that gets you sleeping like a dummy. Toodles!”

**Image prompt:**  
Vertical realistic UGC-style phone shot of the same woman at a warm nighttime vanity mirror, bare face after makeup removal, relaxed and calm, soft lamp lighting, skincare products on the vanity, Gummy Dummy sleep gummy bottle nearby or in one hand, she casually takes one gummy as part of her bedtime routine and gives a subtle playful wink toward the camera through the mirror, cozy authentic bedtime TikTok aesthetic, natural skin texture, no polished commercial look, no exaggerated reaction.

**Motion prompt:**  
Woman continues from the previous scene with the Gummy Dummy bottle already in hand or open on the vanity. She casually takes one gummy, pops it into her mouth while saying the final tagline, then looks into the mirror toward the phone and gives a small playful wink or soft smile on “Toodles.” Hold for a half beat after the wink. No cutaway, no CTA over her face, no exaggerated chewing or reaction.
