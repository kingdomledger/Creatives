## Notes

This was produced in one evening using free / limited AI tools.

Because of the tool limitations, there are visible imperfections:

- Slight color / lighting differences between scenes
- Some camera movement caused by AI video prompting
- Some voiceover timing issues because the video was generated in shorter chunks
- Product handling is imperfect — the gummy appears to be taken out without the container opening
- Generated using Dreamina AI with Seedance 1.5 Pro / legacy-model workflow

The point of this project was not to show a perfect final ad.

The point was to show that I can take a concept from hook → script → storyboard → AI prompts → generated scenes → final short-form ad quickly, and that the quality would improve further with better tools, more generation time, and a single-platform workflow.
