## What this is

This is a smaller, more casual AI video experiment built around a simple character idea: a dog being “interviewed” about bath time like a dramatic street interview.

It is not as elaborate as UGC-v1, but it belongs in the portfolio because it shows a different creative direction: taking a normal pet moment and turning it into a short-form character-driven concept.

## Concept

**Dog interview by the street — AI generated.**

The joke is that the dog narrates bath time like a serious personal incident. The voiceover gives the pet a personality, and the video works because the situation is simple, relatable, and funny.

## Why I included this

This piece is mainly for fun, but it still shows useful creative instincts:

- Turning a simple everyday moment into a character-led short
- Using AI video for comedic timing and personality
- Building a format that can be repeated with other pets / objects / characters
- Testing Seedance 2.0 for quick short-form content generation

## Workflow

1. Start with a relatable pet moment.
2. Give the dog a strong point of view.
3. Write the voiceover like a dramatic street interview.
4. Generate the AI video using Seedance 2.0.
5. Review what worked and what could be improved.

## What I would improve next

With more time, I would:

- Tighten the ending so the joke lands more cleanly.
- Add captions for better short-form retention.
